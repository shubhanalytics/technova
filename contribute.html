Contribute a site

Thanks for contributing! Please provide accurate details and a working URL. Submissions are saved locally and can be pasted into a PR or emailed to the maintainers for review.

Prefer email? Send all required details to shubhank.katarey@outlook.com. Include site name, URL, category, subcategory, owner, and contact email in the message.

Or reach out on X (Twitter): @shubhanalytics

Submissions are stored locally in your browser. To submit to the project, copy your saved JSON into a PR or contact the maintainers.
