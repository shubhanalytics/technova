About Technova

Technova is a lightweight, community-maintained directory for quickly discovering technology projects, companies, programming languages, and developer tools. We prioritize clarity, accessibility, and simple attribution.

Why This Exists

  • Fast discovery: Scan names and short descriptions quickly without information overload.
  • Accessible: Keyboard-first navigation, skip links, and WCAG-compliant contrast ratios.
  • Community-driven: Suggest additions via the Contribute page — no gatekeeping.
  • Privacy-focused: No tracking, no analytics, no cookies.

Categories

Items are organized into clear categories:

  • Tool: Developer tools, utilities, and productivity software
  • Programming Language: Actual programming languages (Python, Rust, etc.)
  • Software: Commercial software products and platforms
  • Framework: Development frameworks and libraries
  • AI/ML: Artificial intelligence and machine learning platforms
  • Startup: Tech startups and emerging companies
  • Cloud: Cloud infrastructure and services

What's Next

We're planning to add curated lists, better tagging, and contribution moderation. Help us grow by suggesting entries via the Contribute form.
