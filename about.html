About technova

technova is a lightweight directory to discover technology projects, companies, and tools at a glance. We focus on clarity, accessibility, and attribution — surfacing short descriptions, domains, and links so you can quickly explore what matters.

Why this exists

  • Fast discovery: scan names, descriptions, and domains quickly.
  • Accessible: keyboard-first navigation, high-contrast colors, and skip-links.
  • Community-driven: contributions welcome via the Contribute page.

What’s next

We plan to add curated lists, tagging, richer filters, and contribution moderation. If you'd like to help, use the Contribute form to suggest additions.
