About technova

technova is a lightweight directory for quickly discovering technology projects, companies, and tools. We prioritise clarity, accessibility, and simple attribution.

Why this exists

  • Fast discovery: scan names and short descriptions quickly.
  • Accessible: keyboard-first navigation, skip-link, and high-contrast colors.
  • Community-driven: suggest additions on the Contribute page.

What’s next

Curated lists, tagging, and contribution moderation are planned. Help by suggesting entries via the Contribute form.
